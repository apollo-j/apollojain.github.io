	<Title>Lasagna</Title>
	<ImageURL>
	http://redirect.bigoven.com/pics/rs/640/lasagna-5.jpg</ImageURL>
	<Ingredients>
		<Ingredient>
			<Name>1 cup (2 sticks) Butter; softened</Name>
		</Ingredient>
		<Ingredient>
			<Name>3/4 cup Brown Sugar</Name>
		</Ingredient>
		<Ingredient>
			<Name>1/4 cup White Sugar</Name>
		</Ingredient>
		<Ingredient>
			<Name>1 small pkg Instant Vanilla Pudding mix</Name>
		</Ingredient>
		<Ingredient>
			<Name>2 eggs</Name>
		</Ingredient>
		<Ingredient>
			<Name>1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract</Name>
		</Ingredient>
		<Ingredient>
			<Name>2 1/4 cups All-Purpose Flour</Name>
		</Ingredient>
		<Ingredient>
			<Name>1 teaspoon Baking Soda</Name>
		</Ingredient>
		<Ingredient>
			<Name>1 pkg ( 12 oz) Milk Chocolate Chips</Name>
		</Ingredient>
	</Ingredients>
	<Instructions>
		Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Beat the butter, both sugars, pudding mix, eggs and vanilla in a large bowl. Beat until creamy and fluffy. Then slowly mix in flour and baking soda. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop by tablespoonfuls, onto an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake for ONLY 9-10 minutes. Remove from oven and let cool about 10 minutes before eating.
	</Instructions>
</Recipe>
